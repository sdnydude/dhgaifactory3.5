# Marketing Plan: Polymyalgia Rheumatica CME Program

## 1. Executive Summary

This comprehensive marketing plan outlines a multi-channel strategy to drive registrations for our Polymyalgia Rheumatica (PMR) CME program targeting rheumatologists and related specialists. With a total budget of $45,000, we project 959 registrations at an efficient $46.92 cost per registration.

### Strategy Overview
Our approach leverages five integrated channels centered around the American College of Rheumatology partnership as the primary driver, supported by targeted journal campaigns, email list rentals, conference follow-up, and peer referral networks. This strategy balances broad reach with precise targeting to maximize engagement within our target audience.

### Key Success Metrics
- **Target Registrations:** 959
- **Budget Efficiency:** $46.92 per registration
- **Primary Channel:** ACR Partnership (30% of budget)
- **Campaign Duration:** 12 weeks with 8-week active promotion

## 2. Target Audience Profile

### Primary Audience: Rheumatologists
- **Estimated Universe:** 5,500 practicing rheumatologists
- **Practice Settings:** Academic medical centers, private clinics, large group practices
- **Geographic Scope:** National

### Secondary Audiences
| Audience Segment | Size | Rationale |
|-----------------|------|-----------|
| Primary Care Physicians | 185,000 | First encounter with PMR patients; need diagnostic clarity |
| Geriatricians | 7,500 | High overlap with PMR demographics; co-management scenarios |

### Key Motivators
- Differential diagnosis challenges between PMR and giant cell arteritis
- Steroid tapering protocols and long-term management strategies
- MOC Part II requirements for rheumatology certification
- Recent guideline updates and treatment algorithm changes

### Engagement Preferences
- **Optimal Timing:** Tuesday-Thursday, 7-8 AM or 12-1 PM
- **Preferred Channels:** ACR communications, specialty journals, professional newsletters
- **Content Focus:** Evidence-based, practical clinical applications

## 3. Key Messages

### Primary Message

### Targeted Supporting Messages
| Target Segment | Message Focus |

| Academic rheumatologists | Implement practical monitoring protocols and long-term management approaches |

**"Register now to enhance your PMR clinical expertise"**
