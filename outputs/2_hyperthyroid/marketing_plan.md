# Marketing Plan: Hyperthyroid Disease CME Program

## Executive Summary

This comprehensive marketing strategy targets endocrinologists through a four-channel approach designed to maximize registration for our hyperthyroid disease CME program. The plan leverages trusted professional relationships through the Endocrine Society Partnership and American Association of Clinical Endocrinologists, while building credibility through Journal of Clinical Endocrinology & Metabolism advertising and driving direct response through Professional Email Lists.

**Key Strategy Elements:**
- Multi-channel approach targeting 559 projected registrations
- $50,000 total marketing investment at $89.45 cost per registration
- 12-week campaign timeline with concentrated push in weeks 3-9
- Focus on MOC Part II requirements and complex case management

**Projected Outcomes:**
- 559 total registrations across all channels
- Primary reach to 6,500 endocrinologists nationally
- Secondary reach to 285,000 primary care and internal medicine practitioners

### Primary Audience: Endocrinologists

**Practice Settings:**

- Hospital-based practices

**Geographic Scope:** National
**Estimated Universe:** 6,500 practitioners

**Key Motivators:**

**Barriers to Engagement:**
- Heavy clinical schedules with limited CME time

- Competition from society-sponsored programs
- Geographic limitations for live events

**Optimal Engagement Times:**
- Weekday evenings 6-8 PM EST
- Early morning 6-8 AM EST
- Weekend mornings

## Compliance Considerations

### Educational Focus Requirements
- All messaging emphasizes educational objectives and clinical improvement
- Content highlights evidence-based practices and patient outcome optimization
- MOC Part II accreditation prominently featured
- No product promotion or commercial bias in educational materials

### Regulatory Compliance
- All communications comply with ACCME standards for commercial support
- Partner communications follow society ethical guidelines
- Email campaigns include required opt-out mechanisms
- Tracking pixels comply with healthcare privacy regulations

### Content Standards
- Clinical accuracy reviewed by medical education committee
- Fair balance maintained in treatment option discussions
- Evidence-based content sourced from peer-reviewed literature
- Case studies anonymized and compliant with patient privacy laws

This marketing plan provides a comprehensive, budget-conscious approach to reaching endocrinologists with compelling, compliant messaging that drives registration while building long-term professional relationships.
