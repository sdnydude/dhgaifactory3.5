# Chronic Cough CME Marketing Plan

## Executive Summary

This comprehensive marketing strategy targets pulmonologists and related healthcare professionals with chronic cough management education through a multi-channel approach. The plan leverages 5 integrated channels with a total budget of **$60,000** to achieve **1,128 registrations** at a cost-per-registration of **$53.19**.

### Strategy Overview
Multi-channel marketing strategy targeting pulmonologists through strategic society partnerships, targeted email campaigns, and conference digital advertising to maximize reach and engagement among chronic cough management specialists.

### Key Channels
- **American College of Chest Physicians Partnership** (18.3% of budget)
- **Pulmonology Journal Email Alerts** (30% of budget) 
- **CME Email House List** (8.3% of budget)
- **Conference Digital Advertising** (33.3% of budget)
- **American Thoracic Society Outreach** (10% of budget)

### Projected Outcomes
- **1,128 total registrations**
- **$53.19 cost per registration**
- **National reach** across academic medical centers and specialty practices

## Target Audience Profile

### Primary Audience: Pulmonologists
- **Practice Settings**: Academic medical centers, specialty pulmonary clinics, multi-specialty group practices
- **Geographic Scope**: National
- **Estimated Universe**: 12,000 practitioners

### Secondary Audiences

| Segment | Size | Rationale |
|---------|------|-----------|
| Primary Care Physicians | 240,000 | First point of contact for chronic cough patients; need referral guidance |
| ENT Specialists | 9,500 | Manage upper airway causes; important for differential diagnosis |
| Nurse Practitioners/PAs in Pulmonary Medicine | 3,500 | Increasingly managing chronic cough in specialty clinics |

### Key Motivators
- MOC Part II requirements for American Board of Internal Medicine
- Clinical frustration with refractory chronic cough cases
- New FDA-approved therapies requiring education
- Patient quality of life improvement opportunities
- Emerging diagnostic tools and guidelines

### Preferred Channels & Timing
- **Channels**: ACCP communications, specialty journal alerts, pulmonary society newsletters
- **Optimal Timing**: Wednesday-Thursday mornings (7-9 AM), Sunday evenings (6-8 PM)

## Key Messages

### Primary Message
**"Advance your chronic cough management with evidence-based diagnostic and treatment strategies."**

### Supporting Messages
- Master the latest diagnostic algorithms for refractory chronic cough evaluation and differential diagnosis
- Learn emerging therapeutic approaches and patient-centered management strategies for complex cases
- Gain practical tools for optimizing patient outcomes in challenging chronic cough presentations

### Call to Action
**"Register now to enhance your chronic cough expertise with actionable clinical insights"**

- **Week -4**: Execute partnership agreements with ACCP and ATS

- **Week -3**: Set up tracking parameters and segment email lists

### Launch Phase (Weeks 1-3)
- **Week 1**: Launch ACCP partnership and first wave email campaigns
- **Week 2**: Deploy Pulmonology Journal email alerts
- **Week 3**: Optimize performance based on early metrics

### Sustain Phase (Weeks 4-10)
- **Weeks 4-6**: CHEST newsletter campaigns and reminder sequences
- **Weeks 6-8**: Peak conference digital advertising during CHEST Annual Meeting
- **Weeks 8-10**: Sustained journal partnerships and society outreach

### Close Phase (Weeks 11-12)

- **Week 12**: Last-chance messaging and campaign wrap-up

## Compliance Considerations

### Educational Focus
All marketing materials emphasize evidence-based content and educational value, avoiding product promotion or commercial bias.

### Regulatory Adherence
- Content reviewed for ACCME compliance
- Fair balance maintained in all communications
- Clear separation of educational content from promotional materials
- Appropriate disclaimers and conflict of interest statements

### Data Privacy
- GDPR and CCPA compliance for email marketing
- Opt-in verification for all email communications
- Secure data handling for registration information
- Clear privacy policy communication

### Society Partnership Guidelines
- Adherence to ACCP and ATS partnership terms
- Approval processes for all co-branded materials
- Proper attribution and branding guidelines
- Ongoing relationship management protocols
