# DHG AI Factory White Paper — Full Plan

**Document:** Multi-Agent Intelligence for Continuing Medical Education  
**Subtitle:** How DHG AI Factory Transforms CME Grant Development  
**Target Length:** 3,500-4,500 words + 10 visual elements  
**Content Ratio:** 45% narrative | 40% visuals | 15% lists

---

## Executive Overview

### Purpose
Position DHG AI Factory as the leading multi-agent platform for CME content generation, demonstrating:
- Technical sophistication without alienating non-technical readers
- Quantifiable business value (time savings, quality improvements)
- Thought leadership in AI-powered medical education

### Target Audience
| Segment | Key Interests | Persuasion Points |
|---------|---------------|-------------------|
| CME Directors | Efficiency, compliance | Time savings, ACCME adherence |
| Medical Education VPs | Strategy, ROI | Competitive advantage, scalability |
| Healthcare IT Leaders | Architecture, security | Robust design, integration points |
| Grant Writers | Quality, workflow | Writing assistance, consistency |
